Concrete Solutions

West Contracting started its General Contracting Division to provide a full complement of services to our clients. The same tenets that made us successful in asphalt paving are the foundation for our success in general contracting work.

Our ability to handle concrete paving projects is second to none. Our services include excavation, roadwork, bridgework, box culverts and sewers. And we work with the state, county and municipal road departments throughout eastern Missouri.
