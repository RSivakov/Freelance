Celebrating 50 Years of Success

  • It's a family affair. Three generations of Wests celebrated the company's milestone. From left: Larry, president, and wife Kathleen, along with son Kyle and daughter Colleen (who works as a project manager), Mary, co-founder of West Contracting, and son Chris.
  • Twenty-three current and past employees show the watch they received for 20 or more years of service with West. This starts a new tradition at West, with all future 20-year employees to receive a watch to honor their service to the company.
  • President Larry West, the second generation to oversee the company, extends his gratitude to West Contracting's more than 150 employees. Larry credits the company's longevity and success to the dedication and hard work of past and current employees.
  • Jim Fitzgerald (left), vice president, presents Larry West, president, with a plaque commemorating the company's 50 years. The plaque frames an article written about West Contracting's storied history.
