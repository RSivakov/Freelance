Work In Progress

West Contracting is working across eastern Missouri to improve roads, parking lots and more. Here are just a few of the contracts awarded to West:

Missouri Department of Transportation(MODOT)

  • Route 44: Phelps County
    $463,767. West Contracting will repair asphalt pavement in Phelps, Crawford and Franklin counties. The total length of the improvement is 52.182 miles.
  • Route 70: Various Counties
    $521,650. Work involves asphalt pavement repair from Route YY to Route T near Warrenton. The total length of the improvement is 42.3 miles.
  • Multiple Routes: Various Counties
    $493,700.00. Asphalt pavement will be repaired in various locations in Jefferson, Franklin and St. Louis counties and in St. Louis City.
  • Route 185: Franklin County
    West Contracting will be a subcontractor for the bituminous pavement (BP-1) at the new bridge at Sullivan, the total length of improvements totaling 1.045 miles.

New Commercial Construction

  • St. Louis Art Museum
    West Contracting has been contracted to move the Serra Sculpture.
  • Lowe’s - Sullivan, MO
    A new Lowe’s is currently under construction in Sullivan, MO, off highway 44. West Contracting has been contracted to complete the parking lot.
  • Purina Farms-Long View Animal Research Facility - Gray Summit, MO
    West Contracting will be constructing a new asphalt parking lot.
  • Missouri Conservation-Little Prairie - Between St. James and Rolla, MO
    West Contracting will be overlaying the existing roadways and parking lot with hot mix asphalt.
  • The Meridian at Brentwood – St. Louis, MO
    New office space at highway 40/64 and Hanley Road. West Contracting will be constructing the new asphalt parking lot.
  • The Meridian Loop/Dale Avenue – St. Louis, MO
    Construction of a new office complex entrance to the Meridian. The work includes excavation, sewer installation, new concrete curbs and road widening with Superpave.
  • St. Louis Galleria
    New asphalt parking lot around the demolition of the existing Lord & Taylor building. Completion set for spring 2009.
  • BJC of St. Peters
    West Contracting is constructing a new asphalt parking lot for the inpatient bed unit and pharmacy. Completion set for fall 2008.

City/Municipality Work

  • McCutcheon Road - Richmond Heights, MO
    Between Highway 40/64 and Clayton Road. West Contracting’s work includes: Full depth pavement reconstruction, new concrete curbs, sidewalks, new storm inlets and sewers, and new decorative street lighting. Work to be completed in the Fall 2008.
  • Franklin County Road Resurfacing
    Approximately $2.8 million. West Contracting is resurfacing approximately 24 miles of various county roads with 2 inches of hot mix asphalt.
  • Franklin County Chip Seal
    Approximately $760,000. West Contracting is resurfacing 20 miles of roadways with trap rock chip seal.
  • City of Sullivan – Franklin County
    $1,200,000. Approximately 2,434 feet of new roadway and approximately 1,000 feet of pavement replacement; water main, sanitary sewer main, earthwork, box culvert, concrete and asphalt paving, and any other incidental work in accordance with Plan and Specifications. The project is located between State Highway AF and the North Service Road of Interstate 44 near the Flying J Truck Plaza in Section 4 & 5, Township 40N, Range 2W, Franklin County, City of Sullivan, Missouri.
  • Old Sugar Creek Road Bridge – Jefferson County
    $646,864. West Contracting is performing roadway improvements and bridge replacement for Jefferson County Public Works.
  • Winghaven Boulevard in O’Fallon
    West Contracting is set to overlay Winghaven Blvd. with Ultra Thin Asphalt (Nova Chip) from Highway 40 north to Highway N in spring 2008.
