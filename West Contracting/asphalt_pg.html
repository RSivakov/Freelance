West Contracting played a part in the Art Museum’s expansion, paving the parking lot. Additionally, the company worked on the roadway and a scenic pathway on Art Hill, one of the most scenic spots in Forest Park, the nation’s second largest municipal park.

Norwood Hills Country Club
