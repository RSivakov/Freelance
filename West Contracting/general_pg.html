McKnight Crossing – Rock Hill
West Contracting is reconstructing the intersection of McKnight Road & Manchester Road in Rock Hill in preparation for the opening of a new shopping center.

Eldon Avenue Improvement, Lakeport to Hollycrest - Maryland Heights
West Contracting reconstructed Eldon Avenue from south of Lakeport Drive to Hollycrest Court. Construction included 30-foot wide concrete pavement, storm sewers, sidewalks, a 3-span precast I girder bridge, and the removal of the existing pavement and bridge.

Rosalie Eulalie Park Walking Trail & Bridge – Brentwood
West Contracting installed a new pedestrian footbridge and associated pathways over Black Creek in this picturesque park.

Weber Rd. & Reynolds Creek Rd. - Jefferson County
West Contracting completed the reconstruction of two existing bridges.

Gravois & Summit Road Intersection - St. Louis County
Just west of Highway 141 on Highway 30, West Contracting is widening the roadway with 9-inch thick concrete pavement to be overlaid with 3 ¾-inch asphalt pavement.
