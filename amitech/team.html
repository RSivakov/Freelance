Leadership Team

The Amitech Solutions team consists of experienced advisors with specialties across a wide range of industries, technologies and practice areas. We provide an integrated advisory approach drawing upon our strengths to deliver strategic and tactical advice, global reach, critical industry knowledge, and operational expertise. We have the ability to draw upon a vast network of diverse professional resources to create collaborative, multidisciplinary teams to provide targeted solutions for our clients. The combination of industry and consulting experience with a case-specific, team approach distinguishes Amitech Solutions from other firms. Each project receives active involvement from senior-level consultants with experience in the industries relevant to our clients' requirements.

President
Principal Consultant

Amit Bhaghat

Amit Bhagat is the Founder, President and Principal Consultant of Amitech Solutions. Amit's principal responsibilities include developing and deploying information management, business intelligence and performance management strategies and solutions. Amit is a well-respected industry thought leader and frequent speaker and has authored several white papers on Master Data Management, Data Governance, and Business Intelligence. Amit's experience over the past 19 years includes design and delivery of complex and innovative information-based business solutions in the healthcare, insurance, and financial services industries to help clients improve decision-making and achieve the next level of performance. Prior to founding Amitech Solutions, Amit worked extensively with clients in healthcare and financial services organizations to develop and implement growth strategies as well as gain measurable improvement in their operational performance using IT-enabled business initiatives.
